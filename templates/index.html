🏠 Home Buying Tips
1

Location is Everything

MedInc (area income) is the #1 factor. Choose areas with good connectivity to IT parks for best appreciation.

2

Check RERA Registration

Always verify RERA registration before buying. It protects you from delays and ensures legal compliance.

3

Super Built-up vs Carpet Area

Carpet area is 30-40% less than super built-up area. Always compare prices on carpet area basis.

4

Metro Connectivity Adds Value

Properties within 1km of metro stations typically command 10-15% premium and see faster appreciation.

5

Budget for Hidden Costs

Add 8-10% for registration, stamp duty, GST and maintenance deposit on top of the property price.
